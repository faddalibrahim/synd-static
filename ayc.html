RATIONALE FOR AYC-SOLUTIONS 2026

As Africa advances toward energy transition, green industrialization, and circular economy models, there is a pressing need for practical, locally driven solutions. Youth across the continent are developing innovations such as clean-tech, climate-smart agriculture, waste-to-wealth enterprises, and green manufacturing.

Yet, systemic barriers, limited finance, mentorship, market access, policy support, and visibility often prevent these solutions from scaling and reaching the communities that need them most.

Participants will have the opportunity to pitch solutions, access seed funding, connect with technical partners, and gain structured guidance to advance their projects. The conference will not only celebrate innovation but also catalyze actionable projects that contribute to sustainable development, job creation, and environmental stewardship.

In this way, AYC-NREG 2026 moves beyond dialogue to accelerate tangible solutions, creating a generation of empowered young leaders capable of shaping Africa’s green and sustainable future.

AYC-NREG 2026 seeks to directly address these gaps by providing a structured platform for young Africans to develop, pilot, and scale their solutions.

The conference will bring together African youth-led startups, innovators, technical experts, investors, policymakers, and development partners in a collaborative environment that promotes knowledge exchange, mentorship, and funding opportunities.

About

African Youth Conference

The African Youth Conference 2026 is scheduled for two (2) days from 22nd to 23rd October 2026 in partnership with the Ministry of Youth Development and Empowerment and is expected to attract approximately 2,000 in-person and virtual participants, including youth leaders and green entrepreneurs from across Africa’s five regions, government officials, civil society organizations, private sector representatives, investors, development partners, academic researchers, and media.

EXPECTED OUTCOMES

  • Youth-led solutions identified and supported through pitching and seed funding with a structured mentorship & incubation pipeline connecting innovators with experts.
  • Improved visibility of African green enterprises, resulting in potential partnerships and market linkages.
  • Strengthened policy advocacy, driven by youth voices shaping natural resource governance and climate transition strategies.
  • Enhanced capitalization of youth-led innovations, with the production of a publication titled “African Youth and Green Solutions: Innovate, Transform, Impact”, extending the conference’s impact and promoting visibility, advocacy, and replication of African youth solutions.
  • Enhanced regional networking, with expanded participation across Africa, beginning with the West African sub-region.

THEMATIC FOCUS FOR SIDE EVENTS

As part of the conference theme “Scaling Youth-led Innovative Solutions for Africa’s Green Economy,” organizations, institutions, youth-led initiatives, private sector actors, and development partners are invited to organize side events that highlight innovative approaches, practical solutions, and collaborative initiatives advancing Africa’s green transition.

Side events should create space for knowledge exchange, partnership building, capacity strengthening, and showcasing scalable youth-led innovations aligned with the thematic areas below.

  • Sustainable Agriculture and Food Systems
  • Circular Economy and Waste Management
  • Green Manufacturing and Sustainable Products
  • Energy Transition
