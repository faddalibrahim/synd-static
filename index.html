Four Pillars. One Mission. A Greener Ghana.

SYND's work sits at the intersection of research, advocacy, and youth empowerment driving climate solutions that are evidence-based, community-rooted, and government-facing.

Youth Climate & NRE Convening Hub

Youth Climate & NRE Convening Hub

This Hub facilitates the coordination, anchoring, and strengthening of multi-level platforms that connect youth, government, civil society, and global partners for collective climate and environmental action. We intend to increase our capacity to host key national, regional and international platforms.

Youth in Natural Resources & Environmental Governance (Youth-NREG) Platform

Youth-NREG Platform

Our Youth in Natural Resources & Environmental Governance (Youth-NREG) Platform drives social change through climate & environmental justice campaigns as well as advocating for good governance practices in the extraction and utilization of our natural resources. Since its inception in May, 2019, the Platform has equipped over 750 young people directly and over 25,000 young people indirectly to lead and engage with NRE policies.

Young Green & Sustainability Entrepreneurs Consortium (YoGSEC)

Young Green Entrepreneurs (YGE)

In addition to the climate campaigns to engage policy makers and educate the public, SYND established the Young Green Entrepreneurs (YGE) programme in 2022 with the aim of identifying and empowering young enterprising people who are innovatively developing green solutions in the fight against climate change and contributing to the circular economy.

Media & Communications

Media & Communications

We are establishing communication and visibility channels on social media including YouTube (SYND TV) and Podcast. This is a strategic amplifier in showcasing youth governance impact, elevating environmental justice stories, and influencing public discourse. This will further strengthen the mission of SYND.
